How to Make Homemade Pizza

  1. Prepare the pizza dough by mixing flour, yeast, salt, sugar, and warm water in a bowl. knead the dough for 5-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  2. Let the dough rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours until it doubles in size.
  3. Preheat the oven to 450F and roll out the dough into a round shape.
  4. Add the pizza sauce, cheese, and toppings of your choice.
  5. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Take the pizza out of the oven, slice it, and enjoy!
